Does it sound like a robot?+

No. Callers hear a natural, friendly voice and have a real back-and-forth. Most don't realize it's AI — they just feel taken care of and get their appointment booked.

Can it handle Spanish-speaking customers?+

Yes. It answers callers in English and Spanish, so you never lose a job because of a language barrier.

What if it can't answer something?+

It takes a detailed message or routes the call to you, and texts you a full summary either way. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Do I have to change my phone number?+

No. We forward your existing number, so your customers call the same number they always have. Nothing changes on your trucks or your signs.

What does it cost?+

Every shop is different, so we price it to your call volume and setup right in the demo — no surprises, no long contracts. Most owners find it costs less than the jobs they're losing each month.

How long until it's live?+

Days, not months. We do the building — you approve it and go. Founding partners get moved to the front of the line.

Is this only for HVAC?+

It's built for HVAC first — your jobs, your scripts, your scheduling, your busy season. That focus is exactly why it works so well for your shop.
